About Code 9

We're a passionate team of innovators, developers, and problem-solvers dedicated to creating exceptional software solutions.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leaders in software development.

Founded in 2015

Started as a small team of three developers working from a shared office space with a vision to deliver quality software solutions.

First Major Client (2017)

Landed our first enterprise client, developing a custom CRM system that became the foundation for our reputation.

Team Expansion (2019)

Grew to 25 full-time employees and moved to our current headquarters, establishing specialized departments.

Industry Recognition (2021)

Received the "Innovative Tech Company of the Year" award and crossed 100 successful projects delivered.

Today

Serving clients across 15 countries with a team of 50+ experts, continuously pushing the boundaries of technology.
